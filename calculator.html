Definitions

Principal Amount ($): The initial amount of money you are investing or saving.

Annual Interest Rate (%): The annual interest rate you expect to earn on your investment.

Compounding Frequency: How often the interest is calculated and added to the principal balance.

Number of Years: The total number of years the money is invested or saved for.

Regular Contribution ($): The amount of money you will add to the investment at each compounding period.
